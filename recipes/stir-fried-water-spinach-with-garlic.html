Stir-Fried Water Spinach with Garlic

Photo by Midori, license CC BY-SA 3.0

A quick and flavorful Indonesian vegetable dish made by stir-frying fresh water spinach with garlic, chili, and seasonings. It's light, savory, and perfect as a side for rice-based meals.

Recipes:

  • 300 g water spinach (morning glory), washed and cut 5-7 cm pieces
  • 3 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 red chilies, sliced (optional)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 50 ml water
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  • Heat the vegetable oil in a wok over medium heat.
  • Add minced garlic and chilies, stir-fry until fragrant.
  • Add water spinach, stir quickly
  • Pour in oyster sauce, soy sauce, and water.
  • Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until the spinach is wilted but still bright green.
  • Adjust salt to taste, remove from heat, and serve immediately.
